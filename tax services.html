Tax Services

We prefer to take a proactive vs. reactive approach to tax services. By keeping current on new tax laws and legislation, we are in a position to identify key tax planning opportunities that minimize both your current and future tax liabilities. We provide our individual and business clients with the taxation expertise and knowledge that they deserve throughout the year.

Manjit Gill, strive to provide our corporate clients with impeccable taxation services. We believe that tax servicing is an art, and an art that we specialize in. We provide our business and individual clients with ideal solutions and services for their financial obligations. Our financial advisors stay consistently up to date with the federal and provincial tax laws, and are hence in an ideal position to review, analyze, and reduce your tax liabilities.
Our services include, but are certainly not limited to:

  • Solving Cash flow problems
  • Increasing profitability
  • Paying less tax
  • Preparing a business plan
  • Methods of buying and selling a business
  • Advising on audits by the CRA
  • Assisting in handling disputes with the CRA
  • Transferring or selling your shares
  • Payroll
  • Ledgers and Journals
  • Year-end T4 summary and supplementaries
  • HST Returns

We understand that personal taxation, as the name suggests is personal and unique to every one of our clients. We know that your needs are varied, and that taxation differs greatly from a student, to a senior citizen, to a new parent, to a newly wed couple, and to an expatriate living in Canada. However, we assure that our offices are more than capable of handling your diverse demands in a timely and effective manner, without compromising on the efficiency of your tax return. We fight to save every dollar of your money, and nothing pleases us more than minimizing your tax responsibilities, and increasing your tax credit opportunities.
Our personal taxation services include, but are certainly not limited to:

  • Income Tax filing for individuals
  • Income Tax filing for college and university students
  • Individual financial advising
  • Financial consultation for self-employed individuals
